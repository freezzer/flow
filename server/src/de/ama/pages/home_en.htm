This Website is a psychological Experiment

it try's to find out, wether anybody can make a realistic assessment of some one else, only by looking at a photograph of this person.

it's not a picture portal for voting pictures.
the categories you vote on, are not like "is the picture nice", "is a person nice", or "is a person sexy".

the Question is,
can a personality be seen only by a small photo?
and do other see your person right by this photo ?

At the other hand you can use this free service for testing a passport-picture and its impression to others. You can verify, wether your self-assessment corresponds to the apraisals of others.



How does it work

you need an acount (name and password). than you can do assessments to the available photographies. if you want to upload a picture , you will be requested to do an apraisal of another photography.

thats it.
later on you can look if other persons have done a apraisals to your photo.
